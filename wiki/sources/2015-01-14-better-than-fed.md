[Original post](../../raw/2015/01/better-than-fed.md)

## Context

This follows the "Jason versus the Fed" series started in September 2014. The ITM with ~5 parameters continues to match or beat predictions from the Federal Reserve's models, which use far more parameters and institutional knowledge. See also [[2015-01-08-two-vs-twentynine]].
