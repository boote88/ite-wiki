## Significance

These animations show the [[information-transfer-model]]'s core mechanism in action: [[supply-and-demand]] are not driven by utility optimization or rational choice, but by the system returning to maximum entropy after perturbation. The "forces" moving prices are [[entropic-forces]] — the overwhelming statistical tendency toward the most likely macrostate.

Blue dots = demand, red dots = supply. Adding/removing dots changes the equilibrium distribution, and the price adjusts as the system re-thermalizes.
