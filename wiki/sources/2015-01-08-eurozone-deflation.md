# Eurozone deflation? (2015-01-08)

[Original post](../../raw/2015/01/eurozone-deflation.md)

A prediction validation post. Eurozone deflation in early 2015 was "not a surprise to anyone following this blog" — the ITM had predicted it in November 2013 (see [[secular-stagnation]], [[cross-country-comparisons]]).

## Notes

- 500€ notes are excluded from the M0 calculation because they are used for cash hoarding, not transactions (see [500€ analysis from Sep 2014](../../raw/2014/09/500-sounds-like-lot-of-money.md))
- The EU number is a weighted average over different countries — individual countries like Spain experience more severe deflation
- The [[information-trap]] predicted this outcome: the Eurozone's [[information-transfer-index]] κ was near the trap criterion, making monetary policy ineffective regardless of ECB actions
