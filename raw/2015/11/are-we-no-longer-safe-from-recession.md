I had a speculative indicator of recessions based on interest rates being high or low relative to the IT model trend based on the NGDP-M0 path all described [at this post](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2014/08/are-interest-rates-good-indicator-of.html). I mentioned that I would check it out when I got a chance given that there seemed to be potential for really low inflation over the next 6 months as I talked about [here](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2015/11/cpi-inflation-predictions-and.html).
