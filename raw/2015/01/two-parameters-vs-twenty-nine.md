Here's an update of the [head-to-head](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2014/09/jason-versus-new-york-fed-dsge-model.html) with the NY Fed DSGE model; the information transfer model (ITM) is looking better every day month ...


![](<media/basic us price level m0 interst rates -compare FRB-US model- smoothed -corrected for quarterly avg-.png>)

The monthly data is essentially following the NY Fed's lower 90% contour, while it is almost inside the ITM's 70% contours. However it is important to remember these contours are for _**quarterly**_ data. But! If the monthly data keeps this up for another quarter or two (and there aren't any revisions), we might be able to declare the ITM the winner early.
