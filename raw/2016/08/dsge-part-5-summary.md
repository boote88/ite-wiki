With the exception of a lack of a coefficient for the first term on the RHS of the last equation, this is essentially the three equation New Keynesian DSGE model: Taylor rule, IS curve, and Philips curve (respectively).



One thing I'd like to emphasize is that although this model exists as a set of information equilibrium relationships, they are not the best set of relationships.
