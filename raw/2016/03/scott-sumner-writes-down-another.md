[Sumner](http://www.themoneyillusion.com/?p=31565)

> _NGDP = MB\*(Base velocity), where V is positively related to nominal interest rates._

Hey, that's a pretty good model. Where have I seen this [before](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2015/08/information-equilibrium-as-economic.html) ...




_V : NGDP ⇄ MB_

_i ⇄ V_



_V = k NGDP/MB_

_log i = α log V_



_log i = α log NGDP/MB +_ _β_ 

_α__β_ _α log k_

This has the added bonuses of using numerical values, showing explicit functional forms, and being fit to actual data (in this case from 2000 to the present).
