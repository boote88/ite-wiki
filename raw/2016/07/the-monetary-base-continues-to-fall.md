The monetary base is continuing its slow fall; I haven't updated [this graph](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2016/01/post-hike-monetary-base-projection.html) in awhile. But first the caveat from [that post](http://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2016/01/post-hike-monetary-base-projection.html):

> _This is probably a sucky prediction anyway since there are only about 6 data points from after the rate hike and the noise (error) has been growing over time. The symmetry argument \[that the fall will be at the same rate/curvature as the rise\] is doing quite a bit of work here._

Anyway, it's not really too bad ...
