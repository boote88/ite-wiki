_Five Years_


The blog is five years old today. It started as essentially an online working paper with [an informal abstract](https://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2013/04/an-informal-abstract.html) (and [addendum](https://informationtransfereconomics.blogspot.com/2013/04/an-informal-abstract-addition-why-now.html)).  The paper I mention never saw the light of day in that form, but morphed into [this paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1510.02435).



In the past year, I wrote both [an Evonomics article](http://evonomics.com/hayek-meets-information-theory-fails/) as well as [a book](https://www.amazon.com/dp/1549599232/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&linkCode=ll1&tag=arandomphysic-20&linkId=560c2d8436cb6d8709609cce96fdb35d). The two most viewed blog posts of the past year were related to the former (essentially a draft version of the _Evonomics_ article along with a continuously updated list of presentations and papers).



Thank you to everyone for reading in whatever form!
